Who we are

International business development for Argentine agroindustrial exports

Abraxas Group develops international commercial opportunities for the export of agroindustrial products originating in Argentina. We are not producers or manufacturers — we operate as international business developers, connecting qualified foreign buyers with established Argentine export capacity and coordinating the commercial and logistical framework of each operation, from the initial enquiry through to contract and shipment.

Origin
Argentina, one of the world's principal agroindustrial origins.
Our role
Independent commercial developers — not a mill or a manufacturer.
Reach
Operations structured for buyers across Asia, the Middle East, Africa and the Americas.

How we operate

Supply & contract framework

We structure supply around each buyer's program. Volumes and delivery schedules are defined operation by operation — we do not publish fixed quantities.

Annual supply contracts

Ongoing programs can be arranged under annual agreements with defined delivery periods.

Volume per operation

Shipment size is agreed case by case with the buyer, based on actual demand and logistics.

Flexible scheduling

Delivery timing aligned to each buyer's demand calendar and destination port requirements.

Availability for any specific operation, quantity or period is confirmed at the time of enquiry and assessed individually.

How we work together

The commercial process

01

Buyer inquiry

Initial contact from the buyer with basic requirements and destination market.

02

Required specifications

We request technical specs, volume, port, Incoterm, packaging and delivery schedule.

03

Technical evaluation

We assess operational availability and confirm feasibility with the Argentine export chain.

04

Commercial offer

A precise, buyer-specific commercial proposal is issued in writing.

05

Negotiation

Terms and conditions are discussed and agreed between both parties.

06

Contract

Formal agreement signed, outlining all commercial and logistical conditions.

07

Shipment

Full coordination from loading at origin through delivery at the destination port.
